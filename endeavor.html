At a glance

Dates: June - August 2019

The goal of the “AgriBio” project at Endeavor Peru is to support and scale-up of agriculture and biodiversity-related industries by developing a pipeline and providing services to qualified high-impact entrepreneurs.

As Project Lead, I refined a six-figure budget, wrote grant proposals, facilitated meetings with key stakeholders and built a pipeline of potential Endeavor Entrepreneur and Scale-Up candidates in the related industries.

About

Endeavor is the world’s leading community of high-impact entrepreneurs. To date, Endeavor has supported over 2,000 entrepreneurs worldwide, which generate combined revenues of over $26 billion, have created over 4.1 million jobs in their local economies and, in 2019, raised over $3.6 billion in capital.
