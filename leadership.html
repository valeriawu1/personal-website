At a glance

I’m passionate about building communities that lead to meaningful relationships, amplify voices of women in tech, and constantly put learning into practice.

Here are some of the communities I love.

Stanford Latino

President

Stanford Latino is the home of the Latin American community at the university. I lead the organization of pre-professional, social, and philanthropic events for 70+ students and alumni to bring the Latino community together.

Stanford Womxn in Design

Director of Design Partnerships

SWID is Stanford’s largest pre-professional organization focused on empowering future design leaders. As Director of Design Partnerships, I facilitated community events and strategic meetings between SWID’s Advisory Board and our 80+ members. I lead a team of six interns focused on bridging design and other disciplines through industry treks and workshops.

Haas Center for Public Service

Peer Advisor and Fellow

Stanford’s Haas Center Cardinal Quarter Fellowship offers Stanford students the opportunity to participate in a full-time, quarter-long public service experience designed to integrate their academic learning with field-based experience. As an advisor for the program, I provided mentorship and pre-field training to students on the 500+ summer opportunities available to engage in local-global public service.

Code in Place (Python)

Section Leader

CS106A Code in Place is an online introductory programming course using the Python language, based on material from Stanford University's established intro course, CS106A. As Section Leader, I facilitated 40-minute Python problem-solving sessions to a cohort of student representing 8+ nationalities.
