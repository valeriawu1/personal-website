About

La Victoria Lab (LVL) is an innovation firm with a mission to improve the lives of the Peruvian emerging class through human-centered innovation. It is backed by IDEO, a multi-national design firm, and Intercrop, a business conglomerate that accounts for more than 3.5% of Peru’s GDP.

Alivia: Bringing affordable healthcare through telemedicine

Guiding question: How might we bring accessible primary care services to uninsured Peruvians in Lima and in rural areas?

We designed a healthcare model that emphasized a patient-friendly, end-to-end service that accompanied the user from booking to delivery of medications at their doorstep. I worked on A/B testing designs on the landing page, scoping new features, designing a frictionless payment system, and improving the conversion rate funnel.

The team entered Stanford’s Hacking 4 Recovery five-day incubator to accelerate our growth and user pipeline.

Tunki: Gamefying e-wallet to leverage user acquisition and growth

We ran low-fidelity, digital experiments through social media to track customer engagement and retention, and researched successful case studies in similar e-wallets.

Our proposal and my designs were presented to the Bank’s top executives.

Purpose Workshop: Recalibrating business priorities in times of crisis

Guiding question: How might we guide Intercorp’s top CEOs to navigate the pandemic using purpose as a compass?

With two seniors from IDEO London, we designed a 2-day workshop to help Intercrop executives recalibrate their priorities, initiatives, and objectives anchored in their purpose. The workshop included fireside chats with various high-level executives like with Hiroki Asai, Global CMO at Airbnb and Jason Baer, Managing Partner at SYPartners.
